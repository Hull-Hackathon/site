Everyone is welcome!

Designers, developer, euntrepreneurs, you’re all welcome! This hackathon is organised to bring people together, and to ship great thing. Don’t be afraid to sign up.

What do we build?

Whatever the hell you want! Teams will likely be split into around 4-6 people, then its up to you folks to put an idea together. Come with your best ones as you’ll need to convince the team.

What about supplies?

We’ve got pizza at the ready, lots of booze, a free breakfast, and everything else you’ll need to keep you fuelled for 24 hours! Did we miss something? Let us know!

I don’t want to be in a team?

Absolutely no problem! We encourage people to come along, hide in a corner, and build their own thing for the 24 hours.

I can't find a team

No problem! Either go alone, or we'll invite you to our Slack chat upon signup where you can chat with everyone attending.
